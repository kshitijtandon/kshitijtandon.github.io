About Me

Welcome to my personal website.I am [Kshitij Tandon], a postdoctoral researcher in the field of microbial ecology. My academic journey commenced with a Bachelor's degree in Biotechnology, which I pursued with great enthusiasm in India. Subsequently, I embarked on a path of further academic exploration, culminating in a Master's degree in Bioinformatics, also completed in India. These formative years laid the foundation for my enduring fascination with the intricate world of microorganisms and their ecological interactions. My passion for microbial ecology found its true expression during my doctoral studies, which spanned from 2015 to 2020. During this period, I pursued my Ph.D. in Bioinformatics at Academia Sinica in Taiwan. It was within this esteemed institution that I had the privilege to delve deeply into the study of microbial communities inhabiting coral reefs and freshwater lakes. My research during this time not only expanded the boundaries of my knowledge but also underscored the critical importance of these microbial ecosystems in sustaining our planet's ecological balance....

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about coral reefs and the incredible marine life that inhabits them.
