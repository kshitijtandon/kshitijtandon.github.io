About me

Hi!!! There and welcome to my personal website.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the School of BioScience, University of Melbourne, working on understand the coral microbiome dynamics

My passion for microbial ecology found its true expression during my doctoral studies, which spanned from 2015 to 2020. During this period, I pursued my Ph.D. in Bioinformatics at Academia Sinica in Taiwan. It was within this esteemed institution that I had the privilege to delve deeply into the study of microbial communities inhabiting coral reefs and freshwater lakes. My research during this time not only expanded the boundaries of my knowledge but also underscored the critical importance of these microbial ecosystems in sustaining our planet's ecological balance....

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about coral reefs and the incredible marine life that inhabits them.
